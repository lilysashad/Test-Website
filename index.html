K-Indie Adventures with Lily

As of December 24, 2020,

I've been struggling to find good, new music. However, it's honestly not difficult to find decent content, especially
since I listen to a bunch of playlists on YouTube. They're usually titled along the lines of "K-Indie/Hip-hop/R&B".
There could be 13 songs in the entire video, but I would always find a gem in my suggested videos.

One gem I was able to find is Endless Summer by CIKI.


Check it out here!

Yesterday, I was jamming out to this song and also low-key set a video of myself jamming to it as my profile video
on social media. Endless Summer is such a feel-good song with a beautiful instrumental. The vocals are unique,
and I will simple say: CIKI's vocals are actually my favorite in the K-Indie genre.

My Favorite K-Indie Artists So Far

1Jeebanoff
2CIKI
3Seo Actor

Jeebanoff is ranked first because I'd heard of him first with his song, Truth. It basically became my anthem for quarantine.
Jeebanoff also released an album recently, titled Talking Book, and I love nearly every track on it. It's incredibly calming and
I could picture the entire album being featured in some drama.

CIKI is ranked second because I've only recently found his works, like Endless Summer and Syndrome. The instrumentals
are pretty and his vocals really suit this genre- it kinda reminds me of singing in italics, but less cringey. I also especially like his
feature on Instant Date by Kumira. His songs are perfect for chill vlogs.

Seo Actor is last, but he's definitely not the worst. I like the melancholic tone he sets in his works- it contrasts with CIKI.
However, in my opinion, his content can be hit or miss. Some songs I like of Seo Actor are August and I'll Be There. Although
Seo Actor is ranked last, I still look forward to his works- I hope he releases an album like CIKI has!
